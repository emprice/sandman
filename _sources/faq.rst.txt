What's with the logo?
---------------------

You have surely noticed that the logo for :rainbow:`sandman` is, truly,
atrocious. This is intended as a lighthearted, tongue-in-cheek reminder
to the user that colormaps based on hue alone look bad and should be
avoided whenever possible. Looking at you, :colormap:`matplotlib.cm.jet`.
